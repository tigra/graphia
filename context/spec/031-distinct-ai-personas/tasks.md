# Tasks: Distinct AI Personas Across the Roster (Spec 031)

Two vertical slices: **Slice 1** adds the standing persona-distinctiveness metric (the
effort-not-results measurement vehicle) and captures the un-threaded "before" baseline;
**Slice 2** makes persona generation roster-aware (backlog option **b**) and captures the
"after" number for the before/after comparison. A follow-up to **spec 016 (AI Character
Personas)** §2.1; pure-Python, no schema/graph/rules change.

> **Slice order is load-bearing:** do **Slice 1 first**. There is no committed
> `persona_near_dup` baseline, so the metric must exist to measure the *current
> (un-threaded)* generation as the "before"; Slice 2 then provides the "after". Each slice
> leaves the app runnable (`uv run pytest -q` green; `make blunder-eval` produces records).
> Independent of other in-flight specs.

Functional spec: `./functional-spec.md` · Technical considerations: `./technical-considerations.md`
